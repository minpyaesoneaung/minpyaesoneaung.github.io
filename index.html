Ikigai is created with inspiration from reading and dissecting hundreds of resources and articles online about choosing the most suitable and effective career path for you.

It is quite common to have difficulties choosing a path for your future. We either face difficulties while deciding between two or more paths, or we feel as though we do not have any dreams or goals. If you’re the latter, you should dedicate some time to reading and doing internships to find what suits you. Even if it’s not your passion, you’ll likely find what sort of qualities in jobs you desire and can build off of that. This website, unfortunately, is more focused for those who have some options for careers they’re considering.

Very important it is to keep in mind that the careers you consider do not have to be traditional ones. Especially in this modern age, you can be as specific with your careers as you want.

The name of our website, "Ikigai", is a traditional Japanese concept that encourages finding a purpose in life through finding a balance between four pillars - passion, mission, vocation, and profession. This YouTuber explains what "Ikigai" means quite well.
